prawn rice

    procedure

  1. Take a large heavy-bottomed pan. Add oil and heat it over medium flame. Then add the onions, garlic, chilli, and red pepper. Saut√© them until they turn soft.
  2. Add the rice to the vegetables. Then add turmeric, salt and pepper. Give the ingredients a mix. Add 400 ml of water and cover the pan with a lid. Lets the rice simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. After 20 minutes, add in the green peas and prawns. Stir the rice and cook it for 4-5 minutes more until the prawns are pink and no longer translucent. Serve hot.
