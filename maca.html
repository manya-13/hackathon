macarons

    procedure

  1. Make the macarons: In the bowl , combine the powdered sugar, almond flour, and ½ teaspoon of salt, and process on low speed.
  2. In a separate large bowl, beat the egg whites and the remaining ½ teaspoon of salt with an electric hand mixer until soft peaks form. Gradually add the sugar . Continue to beat until it become soft.
  3. Add the vanilla and food coloring beat until just combined.Add about ⅓ of the almond flour mixture.
  4. Transfer the macaron batter into a piping bag fitted with a round tip.
  5. Place 4 dots of the batter in each corner of a rimmed baking sheet, and place a piece of parchment paper over it.
  6. Pipe the macarons onto the parchment paper in 1½-inch (3-cm) circles, spacing at least 1-inch (2-cm).
  7. Let the macarons sit at room temperature for 30 minutes to 1 hour, until dry to the touch.Preheat the oven to 300˚F (150˚C).
  8. Bake the macarons for 17 minutes, until the feet are well-risen and the macarons don-t stick to the parchment paper. tansfer them to a wire rack for cooling.
