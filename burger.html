burger

    procedure

  1. chicken, bread crumbs, milk, chopped green onions, Worcestershire sauce and salt and pepper and form into patties.
  2. barbecue or fry for approximately 5 minutes per side or until meat thermometer registers 165°F (74°C).
  3. Serve on a whole wheat bun or in a pita shell garnished with sliced tomato, cucumber, green onion, lettuce and mayonnaise.
