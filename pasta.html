Malaysian pasta

    procedure

  1. Heat a pan. Keep the flame to a low and add 2 tablespoons butter.
  2. When the butter melts,add 1 tablespoon or 2 tablespoons all-purpose wheat flour.
  3. Using a wired whisk, stir the flour quickly as soon as you add it.you get a pale golden color. Don't brown the flour.
  4. Keep the flame on low and then pour 1 cup milk with one hand and stir the sauce mixture with the other.Stir frequently while the milk heats up.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and add the seasonings - ¼ teaspoon black pepper powder, salt as required.
    6. For a cheese sauce, you can add 2 tablespoons of grated cheddar cheese. mix well
  6. Boil 4 cups water with ½ teaspoon salt in a pan.add 1 cup pasta
  7. Then add pasta to the white sauce.
  8. Add ¼ teaspoon each of dried oregano, dried basil and dried thyme. Or add your choice of herbs. At this step you can add some steamed, cooked,roasted veggies if you'd like.
  9. Serve white sauce pasta garnished with some fresh herbs like parsley, chives or dill. You can also garnish with some grated cheddar cheese.
